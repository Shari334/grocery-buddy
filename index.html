Grocery Buddy

Instructions

To add a grocery item, type your item into the input field, and then click the submit button to add your item to the list. Repeat this step until you have created a list to your liking.

Next, you can "check off" completed items by clicking on individual items.

To remove individual items from the list, double click on the item.

To clear an entire list, click the red "clear Items" button.
